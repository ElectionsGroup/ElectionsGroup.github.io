Our Work

The Elections Group has created an array of resources in 2020 to meet the needs and requests of election officials across the nation. We’ve partnered with jurisdictions in California, Georgia, Kentucky, Illinois, Indiana, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and Wisconsin, and the list is growing every month.

Below is a list of sample documents created by our Election Superheroes. These materials are free to download and use (more documents coming soon).

Please contact us if we can customize any of these materials, or provide guidance and coaching to meet the needs of your elections office. Learn how we help.
